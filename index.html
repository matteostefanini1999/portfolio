1. Internship at : Optimal Trajectory Planning for Non-Prehensile Robotic Manipulation

WHAT?

  • Develop an optimized trajectory planning algorithm for non-prehensile manipulation using a collaborative robot (cobot UR5e) to handle objects without gripping.

HOW?

  • Defined the constraints and motion requirements for ensuring no relative motion between the object and the robotic end effector.
  • Implemented and tested the optimization using MATLAB and the CasADi framework, addressing both predefined path planning and point-to-point trajectory.
  • Conducted experimental validation on the UR5e cobot to assess the practical feasibility of the optimized solutions.

RESULTS

  • Successfully demonstrated that the UR5e cobot could achieve optimized non-prehensile manipulation within the defined constraints.
  • Gained significant insights into the dynamics of non-prehensile robotic manipulation and the application of optimization algorithms in robotics.

2. Collaboration with : Optimization of Robotic System for Pharmaceutical Filling Machines

WHAT?

  • Enhance the productivity of a robotic filling system used in pharmaceutical production, increasing the output by 50%.

HOW?

  • Conducted a detailed analysis of the company's robotic filling system.

RESULTS

  • Gained a deeper understanding of the challenges in the packaging industry through collaboration with a world-leading company in the field.

3. Academic project: Packaging System Development for Kinder Pinguì

WHAT?

  • Develop a solution for packaging Kinder Pinguì products into a multi-pack format.
  • Ensure strong attachment without damaging the product surface.
  • Design an automation line that can handle both a 4 and a 6-product multi-pack.

HOW?

  • Defined a morphological matrix to identify the ideal solution.
  • Designed a custom modular conveyor belt using CAD software.
  • Analyzed the web handling for the label peelers.

RESULTS

  • Deployed the complete assembly of the new packaging system.
  • Gained a deeper understanding of packaging processes, material interactions, and the technical considerations required for efficient product handling and presentation.
  • Enhanced teamwork skills through effective communication and coordination with colleagues from various engineering backgrounds.
